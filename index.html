What is Making Way?

Making Way is a fun little game designed for kids approximately 7-13 years of age. The goal of the project is to teach kids about road safety and encourage them to walk to school.

Players are tested on their puzzling skills as they attempt to constantly rearrange the puzzle in order to help the kids navigate the paths to school. Make sure to get all the kids to school before the school starts in order to make your way to the next level!
